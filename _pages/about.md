I am a PhD Candidate at the **Shenzhen VisuCA Key Lab**,
[Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT)](https://www.siat.ac.cn/en/),
Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by
[Prof. Zhanglin Cheng](https://www.siat.ac.cn/en/).

My research focuses on **3D computer vision**, particularly novel view synthesis,
3D Gaussian Splatting, and point cloud reconstruction. I am interested in building
**calibration-free and training-free pipelines** for high-quality 3D scene
reconstruction from sparse or single-image inputs.
